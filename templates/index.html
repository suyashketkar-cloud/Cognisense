Cognisense — A Multimodal Sentiment Analyzer is an advanced AI system that detects and interprets emotions by analyzing multiple types of data simultaneously—such as text, voice, facial expressions—to provide a comprehensive understanding of sentiment.

How It Works

Unlike traditional sentiment analysis that relies only on text, Cognisense combines cues like facial expressions, tone of voice, gestures, and written content. By merging these channels with a Late Fusion strategy, it delivers a more accurate understanding of human emotion—useful for customer service, healthcare, content moderation, and human-AI interaction.

🔬 Why Multimodal?

Human communication is naturally multimodal—we use words, tone, and body language together. A multimodal approach captures this complexity, detecting nuances that single-mode systems miss, such as when someone says "I'm fine" but their tone suggests otherwise.
